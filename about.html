We're Courtyard, a six piece indie-rock outfit from Rochester, New York. Currently, we're working on our debut, seven-track, self-titled album. We enjoy writing bops, getting noise complaints, and playing in basements.

Incidentally, we're also very popular amongst the RIT community for freelance jazz bookings. Let us know if you ever want us at your show (or banquet, ball, gala, party)!
